Alum-Challenges - Extra practice

What is Alum-Challenges?

Alum-Challenges is a project started by a group of enthousiastic alumns of CS50 by Harvard University. Motivated to do something with our newfound skills and keen to give back to the community of CS50; those who came before us, our cohorts and anyone who might come after.

In the spirit of helping each other learn, we decided to start this website to share interesting extra practice problem sets. On here you can find loads of interesting user-generated problem sets to test your skills at programming, whether you're a seasoned CS50 alumn with all the skills to pay the bills, or still struggling with mario.c and looking for extra practice.

This entire website is open-source, and should you feel like it, we welcome community contributions a lot. See any mistakes on the site? Don't wait for us to fix it, but check out our repository on Github and show us what you can do!
